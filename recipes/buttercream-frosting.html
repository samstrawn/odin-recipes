Homemade Buttercream Frosting

Easy-to-Make Frosting to Add a Homemade Touch to Any Dessert!

Perfect for Decorating Cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • food coloring (optional)

Steps

  1. Cream butter in the bowl using a stand mixer until smooth and fluffy.
  2. Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar until fully incorporated.
  3. Add milk and vanilla extract and beat on high for 3 to 4 mins until smooth and fluffy.
  4. Mix in food coloring if desired.
